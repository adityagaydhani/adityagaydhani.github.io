I am a graduate student at University of Minnesota – Twin Cities, where I am pursuing MS in Data Science. I obtained my Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering from University of Pune, India in 2018.

Presently, I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant in the Department of Pharmaceutical Care & Health Systems (PCHS) at the College of Pharmacy. I am advised by Dr. Serguei Pakhomov. My work involves developing a natural language conversational agent for the Minnesota Department of Human Services (DHS) under the MnCHOICES project.

In my free time, I do photography and play guitar. I like listening to music from the Baroque, Classical, and Romantic era; Vivaldi, Mozart, Beethoven, and Chopin are some of my favorite composers. I enjoy solving the Rubik’s cube, playing online chess, and practicing coding problems. I also love to cook!
